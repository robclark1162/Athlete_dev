    • TAYLOR ATHLETE DEVELOPMENT mantra is ‘good for 12 not at 12’, this is a line to describe how I coach. My goal with any young athlete, is to help learn the skills of training for one event with another eye on the future. A life lesson.

    COACHING: I offer a comprehensive programme relevant to the athlete’s chosen event that is aimed to fit into life not take over life.

    I offer at least 2 technical sessions per week over the winter months including strength sessions as well as lone sessions to fit the event. I run 2 tiers of training. TEAM TAYLOR is my group where athletics is their chosen sport and the athlete is competing at a high level. I expect these athletes to be competing regularly. I will only hold 8 places for this. DEVELOPMENT GROUP. This group is usually under 14, new, or learning the sport, athletics is a secondary sport. I’ll loosely max at 12 places COMMUNICATION, All communications will be via parent/caregiver unless permission is given to communicate with the athlete. I run a closed group Facebook page where I (with consent) will post videos of trainings to view and review. HEALTH AND SAFTEY, I am a level 2 first aider.
